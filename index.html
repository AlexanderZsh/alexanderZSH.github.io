About Me

I am a first-year Computer Science Ph.D Student at Singapore Management University, where I am advised by Wang Yong on Data Visualization and Human-Centered Machine Learning.

I derive my Computer Science and Mathematics double degrees from University of California, Irvine.

I research currently focus on Visual Analytics and AI4VIS, but I have broad interest in Data Science related field with emphasis on Data Analysis and Statistics
